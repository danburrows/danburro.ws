Made to Move Sessions

Work —

Web Design, Social Media

Role —

Lead Designer & Art Direction

Client —

Lucozade

Brief —

Lucozade Made to Move Sessions are inclusive weekly workouts streamed live on facebook hosted by famous sports personalities and fitness instructors.

As part of a small team we helped develop an engaging brand, going big with typography and vibrant with colours, attracting the attention of those in the gym or on their lunch break browsing facebook.

Small screen —
Posters —

A series of large format posters were rolled out across gyms nationwide. The designs then re-purposed for a social campaign.
