Ports of Call

Work —

Web Design

Role —

Lead Designer

Client —

Plymouth Gin

Intro —

Plymouth gin’s has a history rich with tales of discovery and adventure. They were keen to keep this tradition going. We created Port of Call, a platform to house these new stories, stories that embodied “The Authentic Spirit Of Discovery”.

With little content to work with we designed and built a blog that was flexible enough to work with various content types. Disciplines working parallel, feeding into each other naturally forming a process of design, build, test, repeat. The result being a fully repsonsive, visually emersive and engaging experience.
